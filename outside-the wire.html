OUTSIDE-THE-WIRE

Outside the Wire is a fast-paced and suspenseful futuristic war film with solid acting and a clear ambition to raise provocative questions about the United States' role in the world. In the near future, a drone pilot sent into a war zone finds himself paired up with a top-secret android officer on a mission to stop a nuclear attack. Initial release: 15 January 2021 Director: Mikael Håfström.
Actors: Anthony Mackie, Damson Idris, Kristina Young, Emily Beecham, Piou Asbaek.
Age: PG 16 L.
Showing at: 23:45PM
