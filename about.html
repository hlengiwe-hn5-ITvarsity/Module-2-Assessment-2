About

Pine City Mall, an expansive mall with brand-name, fashion & footwear retailers, casual eateries & a movie theater. It is a shopping mall located in Waterfall City, Midrand, Gauteng. It is the largest single-phase shopping mall to be built in Africa, but smaller than Gateway, Sandton, Menlyn and Fourways Mall. The total retail area is 131 000 square metres, and a gross area of 550 000 square metres. The shopping mall has four main levels of shopping as well as an outdoor restaurant plaza which connects directly to the Waterfall City Park.
