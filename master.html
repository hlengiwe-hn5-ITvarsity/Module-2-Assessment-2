MASTER

Master is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, who also wrote the screenplay and dialogues by Kanagaraj with Rathna Kumar and Pon Parthiban. JD, an alcoholic professor, is enrolled to teach at a juvenile facility, unbeknownst to him. He soon clashes with a ruthless gangster, who uses the children as scapegoats for his crimes. Initial release: 13 January 2021 (India) Director: Lokesh Kanagaraj Producer: Xavier Britto
