RISE-UP

In an effort to reclaim their bodies and lives, a group of women explores the intersections of movement and meaning in a powerful pole-dancing programme. Every dancer is different, but the cathartic course brings them all together. Initial release: 5 February 2021 Director: Michele Ohayon Screenplay: Michele Ohayon Producer: Michele Ohayon Music composed by: Lili Haydn.
Cast: Sheila Kelley, Jenyne Butterfly, Amy Bond Age: PG 16 VLS Plays at: 21:00PM
