OK Furniture is a one-stop affordable furniture and electronics retailer in South Africa and belongs to the retail group Shoprite Holdings. As a whole, the company has 1855 corporate stores, 359 franchises, and stores are located across 15 countries in Africa and the Indian Ocean Islands. To date, there are 387 OK Furniture stores conveniently located in most shopping centres. Fridges, beds, TVs, audio, flooring, outdoor furniture and multimedia devices belong to the most popular product categories.
